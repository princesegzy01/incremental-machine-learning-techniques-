VIDEOS: Group takes Nigerian dances to Vienna Fashion Week runway
Videos showing a dance crew, Gold Caviar Crew on the runway of the Vienna Fashion Week has gone viral on the internet.

In the videos, the group, whose Instagram bio describes as “Bringing #Afrodance & Good Vibes to Vienna ✨ ” were seen on the runway dancing some popular Africa dance steps, like Shaku shaku, Zanga, Gwara gwara etc.

The videos have been well received across different social media platforms.
