Drizzle teams up with Mayorkun in Bola
Olushola Ricketts

Adedayo Mustapha, also known as Drizzle, has returned with a duet with Mayorkun.

Produced by Dapiano, the song is titled Bola.

Describing the new song, Drizzle said, “Bola is a beautiful love song about how I want my woman to enjoy good love and money. Mayorkun also blessed the song with his catchy lyrics.”

The Sugar Daddy Music signee stated that the video had been shot and was directed by Mr C.
