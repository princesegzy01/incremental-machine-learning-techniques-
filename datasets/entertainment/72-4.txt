Cheekychizzy debuts Kokoma
Olushola Ricketts

Singer and producer, Chisom Ifeachor, aka Cheekychizzy, has released a new single, Kokoma. The song was recently premiered by the music giant, Sony Music, alongside his record company, DB Records, which is owned by D’banj.

Cheekychizzy, who is an only son, said he produced his first song when he was eight. He stated, “I have been playing musical instruments since I was a kid. But I started music professionally in 2006. As a producer, I’ve produced songs for D’Banj, Wande Coal, Kayswitch, Davido and Mayorkun.

The producer insisted that he ensured his songs always told stories that would make the listener want more. He stated, “I draw inspirations from everyday happenings and any good music. I have performed on some of the biggest music stages and top festivals in the world. Recently, I performed at the Global Citizens Festival in South Africa with my boss, D’Banj.”

According to him, his song, Lemonade, on which he featured D’Banj, made a massive impact online and across all streaming platforms. “Things have been good for me. I recently secured a deal with Sony Music Africa through DB Records/DKM Music. I just released a new single, Kokoma, which was premiered by the world Sony Music and DB Records. I plan to release more projects and collaborations before the year ends, including a body of work,” he said.
