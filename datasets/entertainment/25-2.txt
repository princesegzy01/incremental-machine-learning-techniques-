2019 BBNaija: I need my girlfriend back, Tacha’s boyfriend tells Biggie
Tasha’s boyfriend, popularly known as King Ladi has pleaded with the organisers of the Big Brother Naija reality show to end the show, so he could be reunited with his girlfriend.

He said this via a post he shared on his Instagram page @kingladiofficial, where he attached a crying emoji saying he needed his girlfriend back home.
