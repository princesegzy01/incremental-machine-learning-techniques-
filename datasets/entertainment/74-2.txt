Wale Adenuga’s Knockout goes to cinemas
Olushola Ricketts

Wale Adenuga Productions’ new comedy movie, Knockout, will hit the cinemas across Nigeria and Ghana on Friday, April 19.

Meanwhile, the movie trailer has been enjoying a lot of views on social media platforms such as Instagram, YouTube and Facebook.

The producer of the movie, Wale Adenuga Jnr, maintained that he was certain it would do well when it was released. He stated, “We really appreciate the large number of views and positive comments we’ve got so far from people who have seen the trailer. It is a good indicator that movie lovers across Nigeria and Ghana understand the importance of the movie. We want it to be the biggest and funniest movie in the history of Nigeria.”

The movie features an array of actors including Sola Sobowale, Charles Okocha, Toyin Abraham, Chinwetalu Agu, Odunlade Adekola, Patience Ozokwor, Broda Shaggi, Ali Nuhu, Eniola Badmus, Papa Ajasco, Mama Ajasco, Pa James, Alinco, Miss Pepeiye, Ajasco, Francis Odega, Afeez Oyetoro, Nnenna, Desmond Elliot, Funky Mallam, Kenny Blaq, Ngozi Nwosu, Gentle Jack, Remi Surutu, Victor Osuagwu, Woli Arole, Yaw, Tayo Amokade, Klint Da Drunk and Segun Arinze among others.
