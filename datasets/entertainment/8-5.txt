Mercy Johnson-Okojie inks new deal
Mobola Sadiq & Toluwalope Kareem

Actress, Mercy Johnson-Okojie, is now a brand ambassador for a milk company.

The company stated that Johnson-Okojie perfectly embodied the brand’s values.

The company’s Managing Director, Deepanjan Roy, stated that Johnson-Okojie’s passion was evident in everything she did, and she truly embodied the ideal qualities that the brand wanted to be associated with. “We have chosen Mercy Johnson-Okojie to be the face of our brand as we believe she will inspire a much larger audience to understand and adopt the product for its quality nutritional benefits, convenience and value for money. We are thrilled to have her as our ambassador,” he said.
