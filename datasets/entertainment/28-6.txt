Brewery rewards winner of Hi-Life Festival with N5m
Tony Okafor, Awka

Chijioke Charles, also known as Ceejay, has been named as the Best Highlife Singer in the South-East and South-South geopolitical zones.

Chijioke defeated three other artistes in a keenly contested competition, tagged Hi-Life Festival and sponsored by Nigeria Breweries Plc, brewers of Life Lager Beer.

For emerging winner of the competition in the singing category, he received a cash prize of N5m, while the first runner-up, Frank Onyeka, got N2m and the second runner-up, Umunnakwe Nwajiaku, went home with N1m.

Speaking at the Grand Finale of the event held in Onitsha, Anambra State, the Portfolio Manager, Mainstream Brands, Nigeria Breweries Plc, Omotunde Adenusi, expressed satisfaction with the success of the competition and the bond, which Life beer had created among talented youths in the South-East and South-South regions.

He said, “Life lager beer is all about progress. Giving these talented acts a platform to showcase their talents has been a thing of joy for us.

“We believe that everyone, who has been on this show, is a winner and we look forward to seeing how they progress in their careers.

“We also thank our loyal consumers for constantly coming out to support our contestants.

“We have thoroughly enjoyed sharing these nights with our consumers and they can expect more of these in the coming years”.
