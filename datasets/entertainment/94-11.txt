Naomi Campbell, boyfriend Liam Payne attend Davido’s London concert
Supermodel Naomi Campbell and musician boyfriend Liam Payne have been seen at the London concert of Nigerian music star David Adeleke, aka Davido.

The 48-year-old Ms Campbell and her 25-year-old beau have reportedly been dating for about two month now.

Liam Payne is one of the musicians with One Direction, the English-Irish pop boy band based in London.

The Evening Standard reports that the pair have been spotted on a series of dates earlier.

The pair were spotted at a Davido concert on Sunday, Evening Standard has said.

“Fans saw them at the London O2 Arena enjoying the Nigerian singer’s performance from a VIP suite,” the newspaper added.
