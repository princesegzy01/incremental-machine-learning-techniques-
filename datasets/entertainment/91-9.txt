RMD’s sweet Valentine message to wife, Jumobi
Popular actor, Richard Mofe Damijo, in the spirit of Valentine’s Day celebrated his wife, Jumobi, on his Instagram page.

Sharing their photo, RMD, as he’s fondly called, revealed the first words, that he said to his wife, some 22 years ago.

He said, “Adejumobi. Abike. Abiks. Abk. Adumaradan…. See how far we have come… I still don’t deserve you. First words I said to you when I saw your heart for me over 22 years ago, and until this minute I still don’t feel I deserve you. Thank you for always.”
