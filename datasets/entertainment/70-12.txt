Chidinma becomes Molped brand ambassador
Ademola Olonilua and Timileyin Akinkahunsi

Music sensation, Chidinma Ekile, has been unveiled as the brand ambassador for Molped sanitary pad made by Hayat Kimya Holdings.

The 2010 Project Fame West Africa winner while being unveiled as the new face of the sanitary towel and Molfix pants for children in Lagos earlier in the week, expressed hope that the new products will not only offer comfort to users but also boost their confidence levels.

The star musician, who thrilled guests at the event with some of her hit songs including the popular ‘Kedike’, said that, “I am happy to be the newest member of the Hayat Kimya family and this is because of Molped sanitary pad which is a globally celebrated product for women.

“I have tried it and see how comfortable it is. I am positive that other women will find it very convenient as well.”

Managing Director of Hayat Kimya, Hakan Misri, said they are delighted to partner with the Afro-pop singer to promote the new products as a result of her impressive achievements in the Nigerian entertainment industry.

According to him, the 27-year-old lady’s beauty, strength and boldness speaks volumes of the amazing qualities of Nigerian entertainers especially women. ation of my upbringing and environment. I always like to look comfortable and elegant,” she added.
