Niniola, Alex Ekubo, Moesha Boduong, others shut down Silver Fox
Olajide Samuel

With a promise to constantly provide top-notch adult entertainment and ease up the stress of living in a city like Lagos, Silver Fox luxury adult entertainment club recently hosted high-profile guests to yet another night of exquisite fun.

On Saturday, September 28, the club launched its exclusive indoor pool/lounge with an exclusive pool party and mini-concert. With special appearances by models including Moesha Boduong, Yazzi Stush, Dezel, and others, it surely was a night for stars.

The crowd lit up with excitement as the night reached its crescendo with ‘Bana’ singer, Niniola, rousing all present with her many groovy singles.

Commenting on the success of the party, the CEO of Silver Fox, Big Slim stated, “At Silver Fox, we avail our guests all the possibilities that they can think of, with the highest standard. We guarantee satisfaction at all times.”

Other Nigerian celebrities present at the exclusive event were Larry Gaga, Alex Ekubo, W4, and a host of others.
