VIDEO: Don Jazzy, Dr Sid visit BBNaija House
Mavin Records CEO, Michael Collins Ajereh, popularly known as Don Jazzy; and fellow entertainer, Sidney Onoriode Esiri, aka, Dr Sid, visited the Big Brother Naija house on Saturday.

The visit took place a few minutes before the Indomie cooking challenge commenced.

“Don Jazzy will be the indomie task master for the cooking challenge,” a memo from Biggie states.

Excited housemates took the two visitors round the house.
