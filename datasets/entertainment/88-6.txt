2019 Elections: Femi Otedola, daughter DJ Cuppy vote
Billionaire, Femi Otedola and his daughter, Florence, popularly known as DJ Cuppy, on Saturday, exercised their franchise in the presidential and National Assembly elections.

The father-daughter duo shared photo and videos on their verified Instagram handles.

While Otedola captioned his video, “Election Day voting with my daughter @cuppymusic”, DJ Cuppy said, “Already at our local queue READY TO VOTE with @FemiOtedola”
