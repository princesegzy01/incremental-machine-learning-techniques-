Veteran filmmaker, Eddie Ugbomah, dies
A veteran filmmaker, Eddie Ugbomah, has died.

Ugbomah, aged 78, died in Lagos on Saturday after a protracted illness. The deceased, scheduled for a surgery on Monday, succumbed to death less than 72 hours to the surgery time.

The news of Ugbomah’s death was broken on the Facebook page of Shaibu Hussein, a member of a committee set up to raise fund for Ugbomah’s treatment.

It read, “I have the permission of the Chair of the Chief Eddie Ugbomah Medical Fund Committee Alhaji Adedayo Thomas (DG, NFVCB) to break the news of the passage into eternity of the veteran filmmaker Chief Eddie Ugbomah, OON.

“The veteran filmmaker died an hour ago at the hospital where he was scheduled to undergo a surgery on Monday. Sad, but t we totally submit to the Almighty.”
