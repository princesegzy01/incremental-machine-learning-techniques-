‘Nollywood Stars Pop-Up’ back on DSTV, GOTV
Mobola Sadiq & Toluwalope Kareem

Africa Magic is turning up the heat and making this October one to remember with the return of the Africa Magic ‘Nollywood Stars Pop-Up’ channel on DSTV and GOTV.

Launching on Sunday, October 6, and running till Wednesday, November 6, 2019, the channel ‘will take all active customers of certain bouquets on a roller-coaster ride filled with drama, comedy, suspense, and intrigue from the biggest Nollywood titles, 24 hours and seven days a week with no repeats’.

Channels Director, Africa Magic, Wangi Mba-Uzoukwu, said the return of the channel would be a treat for the eager audience this October, offering uninterrupted Nollywood entertainment at no extra cost. “We are undoubtedly the home for first-class movies and television series from Nollywood.”

Stars who will grace the screens on the pop-up channel include Omotola Jalade-Ekeinde, Rita Dominic, Majid Michel, Deyemi Okanlawon, Blossom Chukwujekwu, and many others.
