Kemi Lala makes directorial debut with Lavender
Ademola Olonilua and Timileyin Akinkahunsi

Following the success of her one-woman show – ‘Naked’, which premiered at the Lagos Theatre last year, starlet, Kemi Lala Akindoju, will be making her directorial debut at the same festival this year with ‘Lavender’.

‘Lavender’, a stage play presented by The Make It Happen Productions, is owned and run by Kemi Lala Akindoju. The play stars Kehinde Bankole, Deyemi Okanlawon, Oludara Egerton-Shyngle, and Rita Edward.
