Music promoter, Danku, buys Benz for artiste
Popular music promoter, Adepitan Yusuf, aka Ijoba Na Danku, has bought a Mercedes Benz car for his artiste, Oluwaseun.

Danku was said to have bought the car to surprise the artiste who has been very loyal to him over the years.

“I’m very happy about this gift. Danku has been very supportive since we started together. He remains my role model. I will never disappoint him,” Oluwaseun said.

Meanwhile, Danku and Oluwaseun, in collaboration with Dan Marlic, have released a new music video titled, Ijoba Federal, directed by Fizzle & Bizzle.

Danku has worked with other artistes such as D’banj, Oritse Femi, Jigan Babaoja, Rayce, among others.
