2019 BBNaija: Don Jazzy, Rita Dominic, others react to Mercy’s win
Nollywood actress, Rita Dominic; singer, Don Jazzy and others have reacted to Mercy Eke’s emergence as winner of the 2019 Big Brother Naija reality show.

The News Agency of Nigeria reports that Mercy was declared a winner after polling over 41.77 per cent of the votes and went home with N60 million.

The celebrities, who took to their twitter pages to react on the just concluded BBNaija reality show, also hailed Mercy’s prowess as the first female to have won the coveted prize.

The Mavin boss congratulated the winner and also reacted to the extraordinary ass (Yansh) of Mercy.
