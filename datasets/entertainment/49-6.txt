Bollywood channel, StarLife, debuts on StarTimes
Tofarati Ige

Digital TV operator, StarTimes, has announced the addition of popular Bollywood Channel, StarLife, to its list of channels specially for the entertainment of its subscribers who enjoy content from the Indian film and TV industry.

Addressing a press conference recently, the Brand and Marketing Director of Startimes, Qasim Elegbede, said StarLife would showcase only the best family dramas, blockbuster movies, series, celebrity dance shows and more on Channel 500/550.

The Content Marketing Manager of the company, Bose Adewara, noted that all the programmes on StarLife were relatable to practical life in Nigeria and were in English Language, as such making it easy for Nigerian audience to follow.
