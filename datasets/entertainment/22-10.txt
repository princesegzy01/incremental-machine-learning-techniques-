VIDEO: Ooni dances ‘Audio Money’ with Paul Okoye
Ooni of Ife, Oba Adeyeye Enitan Ogunwusi, Ojaja II, rocked it with Paul Okoye of the defunct P-Square Group as the monarch engaged in a sing-along with the musician.

The royal had rocked to the tune of Paul’s new song, Audio Money.

Date was Friday (today), and venue was the Ooni’s Palace in Ile-Ife.
