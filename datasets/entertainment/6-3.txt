Seyi Law to thrill at Afrisafe awards
Mobola Sadiq & Toluwalope Kareem

Popular comedian, Seyi Law, has promised to deliver top notch entertainment for attendees of the upcoming Afrisafe awards, which he is billed to host. He will be joined by Ugochi Obidiegwu, popularly known as ‘the Safety Chic’ and Amazing Adeboyin.

More than 300 industry leaders from four African regions will be treated to a night of glitz and glamour at the event that will celebrate excellence in health, safety, environment and wellbeing initiatives in Africa.

Speaking on what fans should expect from him at the event, Seyi Law said, “Whenever I am on stage, I always give my best. It doesn’t matter whether it’s a concert or awards ceremony, the goal is to make people happy.”

The Chief Executive Officer of HSENations, organisers of the awards ceremony, Femi Da-Silva, said the initiative is primarily to reward excellence. “Afrisafe is an annual award project that is committed to celebrating the best of health and safety conscious organisations, professionals and practitioners, and their achievements in keeping facilities and lives safe.”

Awardees expected at the event include the founder of Juli Plc and Chairman of MTN Foundation, Prince Julius Adelusi-Adeluyi, among others.
