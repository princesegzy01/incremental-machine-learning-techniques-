Danfo Driver musical group’s ‘Mad Melon’ is dead —Daddy Showkey
Nigerian Reggae singer, Omeofa Oghene, known as “Mad Melon” from the popular Danfo Driver music group is dead.

Veteran singer and social activist, Daddy Showkey announced this via his Instagram page on Thursday, September 2019.

Daddy Showkey said, “Can somebody tell me that is not true that we just lost Mad Melon DANFO Driver pls”.

Mad Melon was part of the popular group ‘Danfo Driver’ which also had Mountain Black as a member.

The group became popular in the early 2000s for their unique music style which till date has remained admired by Nigerians.
