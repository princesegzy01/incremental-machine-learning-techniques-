Chika Lann debuts with ‘The Millions’
Tofarati Ige

Actress and social media personality, Chika Lann, is set to make her directorial debut with the release of her new movie, ‘The Millions.’

Starring top Nollywood acts such as Ramsey Nouah, Ayo Makun, Toyin Abraham, Blossom Chukwujekwu, Ali Nuhu, Nancy Isime, among others, the film tells the story of a charismatic con man, who enlists the services of three other scammers, to carry out the ‘biggest heist in history’. However, things didn’t go as planned as they all had ulterior motives for being on the job that would see the winner take all, while the losers fight for their lives.

Written by Tunde Apalowo, the film was shot across Nigeria in various locations including Kaduna, Abuja and Lagos. It is due to start showing in cinemas at month end.
