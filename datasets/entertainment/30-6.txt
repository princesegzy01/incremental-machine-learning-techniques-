AU, AFRIMA unveil nominees
The African Union, and the international committee of the All Africa Music Awards, has unveiled the nominees’ list (regional category) for its sixth edition.

The regional category for best female artistes and best male artistes of the five regions (western, southern, eastern, northern, and central Africa) were thoroughly selected from the entries to arrive at the artistes who would compete at the 2019 awards ceremony.

This year’s list showcases a wide range of creativity and talents both from new and known artiste across the regions. Artistes such as Shatta Wale, Kizz Daniel, Salif Keita, and Burna Boy will be competing for the Best Male category in Western Africa, while Teniola, Simi, Aya Nakamura, Mzvee, Yemi Alade, among others, will compete for the Best Female category in Western Africa.

The organisers also stated that the continental category comprising different genres of music would be released on Friday, August 23, following an auditing process from the international auditors of AFRIMA.

The online voting platform for the awards will be opened to the public on Sunday, September 1, and will run till November 22, where both continental and regional nominees in the 36 awarding categories will compete against one another for the coveted 23.9 carats gold-plated AFRIMA trophy.
