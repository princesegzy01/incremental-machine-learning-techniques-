Saddamopiee bags best collaboration gong at ASE Awards
South Africa-based Nigerian artiste, Opeyemi Adigboluja, also known as Saddamopiee, has won ‘Best Collaboration’ award at the African Social Entertainment Awards in South Africa.

Alongside artistes like Casper Nyovest, Kwesta, Buffalo Soujah and Slim Case, Saddamopiee was nominated in seven categories.

He, however, won the award for Best Collabo for his song, Ogenebo, featuring DJ Yemite.

At the ceremony, all eyes were on Saddamopiee after he gave an energetic performance and an exciting speech.
