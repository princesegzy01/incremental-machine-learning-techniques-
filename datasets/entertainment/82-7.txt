Davido hits 10 million followers on Instagram
Award-winning singer, David Adedeji Adeleke, popularly known as Davido has hit 10 million followers on Instagram.

Instagram is a social networking application that enables users to capture, share and edit photos, videos and messages with friends and their followers

With his 10 million followers, the “FIA” crooner thus became the first Nigerian artist achieve the feat.

The News Agency of Nigeria reports that the DMW boss took to his official Instagram account to celebrate this achievement and said that he would “post every win for everyone to see.”

The 26-year-old artist had set such record of firsts previously.

He was the first Nigerian artist to garner one million followers on Instagram.

His 2017 hit song, ”Fall” became the first Nigerian music video to hit 100 million views on YouTube.
