Useni appeals verdict on Plateau gov election
James Abraham, Jos

The governorship candidate of the Peoples Democratic Party in Plateau State, Senator Jeremiah Useni, has filed 21 grounds of appeal before the Court of Appeal in Jos giving reasons why his petition asking the state governorship election petitions tribunal to upturn the victory of Governor Simon Lalong should be upheld.

Useni’s Media Consultant, Mr Yiljap Abraham, confirmed the development in a statement on Monday.

Abraham said, “Useni’s legal team, led by Chief Mike Ozekhome, SAN, and Edward Pwajok, SAN with Benson Igbanoi, Chief Sunday-Gabriel Odey and 10 other legal experts, has filed the notice of appeal and formulated 21 grounds of appeal to overturn the October 2, 2019 verdict of the governorship election petitions tribunal dismissing his petition.”
