Goje’s daughter, 17 others make Yahaya’s list
Chima Azubuike, Gombe

Governor of Gombe State, Alhaji Inuwa Yahaya, has forwarded the name of Dr Husseina Goje, daughter of Senator Danjuma Goje, and 17 others as commissioners-designate to the state House of Assembly.

In a letter read by the Speaker, Abubakar Kurba, during plenary on Wednesday, the governor also nominated Goje’s son in-law, Ibrahim Waziri, as a commissioner.

Goje, who governed the state between 2003 and 2011 and serves as leader of the All Progressives Congress in the state, is said to have been instrumental to the emergence of the governor.

Other nominees include Dr Ahmad Gana, Dr Aishatu Maigari, Zubairu Umar, Mohammed Gettado, Mr Julius Ishaya, Mrs Naomi Awak, Abubakar Bappah, Muhammad Maigari, Usman Biri, Adamu Dishi, Ibrahim Alhassan, Dr Habu Dahiru, Dauda Zambuk, Mijinyawa Yahaya, Mela Nunge and Meshak Lanco.
