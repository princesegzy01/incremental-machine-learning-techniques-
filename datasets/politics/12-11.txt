Katsina Assembly confirms Buhari’s niece, others as commissioners
Olaide Oyelude, Katsina

President Muhammadu Buhari’s niece, Rabiatu Muhammed, was among the 17 nominees the Katsina State House of Assembly confirmed as commissioners on Wednesday.

Muhammed, who hails from Daura Local Government Area, is the daughter of the President’s late elder brother.

The other nominees confirmed as commissioners included Musa Funtua, Usman Gwajo-Gwajo, Tasiu Dandagoro, Abdullahi Imam, Dr Rabe Wasiu, Faruk Zobbe, Mukhtar Gidado, Abdulkadir Danladi and Badamosi Lawal.

Others were Abdulkadiri Zakka, Usman Nadada, Kassim Mutallah, Abdulkarim Yahaya, Mustapha Mahmud, Yakubu Danja and Hamza Zuleima.
