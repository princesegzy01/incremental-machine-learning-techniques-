Prepare to work, Ihedioha tells aides
Chidiebube Okeoma, Owerri

Governor Emeka Ihedioha of Imo State on Wednesday charged his appointees to justify their appointments into the state executive council.

Ihedioha, who spoke while declaring open a three day retreat for his appointees, said his administration was indebted to the people of the state.

He said that the rots he met on the ground on assumption made it imperative that every appointee must prove that his appointment was justified.

He said, “This retreat is vital to us because it will provide us the opportunity to talk to ourselves unencumbered

“The task ahead of us is enormous and the expectations of our people are indeed huge, thus the need for us to pause and appreciate these challenges.

“The importance of this retreat is to create the needed bonding and build a team with vision and strategize to achieve the rebuild Imo project.

“Aso we are here to find solutions that are deliverable and timely. I strongly advise we take this retreat seriously”
