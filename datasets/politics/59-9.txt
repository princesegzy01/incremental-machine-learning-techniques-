Kogi gov poll: Yahaya Bello wins APC primary
The governor of Kogi state, Yahaya Bello, has emerged winner in the primary of the All Progressives Congress held on Thursday in Lokoja, the state capital.

Out of a total of 3,596 delegates that were accredited, Bello had secured 3,127 votes.

Bello will represent the party in the November 16 governorship election.
