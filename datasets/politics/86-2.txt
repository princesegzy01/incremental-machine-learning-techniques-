Makinde nominated ex-lawmakers, profs, other as commissioners
Ademola Babalola, Ibadan

Four former lawmakers in the Oyo state House of Assembly, Kehinde Ayoola, Funmilayo Orisadeyi, Ojemuyiwa Ojekunle and Adeniyi Farinto were among the 14 nominees for commissioners in the state sent by Governor Seyi Makinde to the assembly.

The list sent to the Speaker, Adebo Ogundoyin, by Makinde was read on the floor of the assembly on Thursday morning.

Orisadeyi, from Ibarapa land, is the only female nominee.

Others are a former caretaker chairman, Ibadan Northwest Local Government Area, Mr. Wasiu Olatubosun; Prof. Oyewo Oyelowo, Prof. Daud Sangodoyin, Mr. Akinola Ojo, Adeniyi Adebisi, Lasunkanmi Ojeleye; Seun Asamu; Rahmon Abdulraheem, Chief Bayo Lawal and Dr Bashir Bello.

The Speaker directed all the nominees to submit 14 copies of their CV on or before August 2 for screening.

He said screening date would be announced later.
