Tribunal upholds election of Kano APC Reps member
Ted Odogwu, Kano

The National and State House of Assembly Tribunal sitting in Kano, on Wednesday, struck out the petition, instituted by the candidate of the Peoples Democratic Party, Usman Adamu, challenging the victory a House of Representatives member, Abdullahi Gaya of the All Progressive Congress.

Gaya was declared winner of the election representing Ajingi/Gaya Federal Constituency.

The three-member panel, led by Justice Ajoke Adepoju held that the petitioner failed to prove the allegations bordering on corrupt practices, over-voting and allocation of votes to non-participating parties.
