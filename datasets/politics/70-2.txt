Police inspector killed in Kano
Ted Odogwu, Kano

Unidentified persons on Thursday stabbed a policeman, Inspector Isiyaku Babaji, to death at his house in Kwari Sabuwa in Kano State.

It was learnt that the intruder had knocked on the door of the policemen and stabbed him with a dagger as soon as the man opened the door.

The state police command spokesman, DSP Abdullahi Haruna, could not be reached as of press time.
