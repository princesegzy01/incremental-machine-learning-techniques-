Nasarawa Assembly confirms Sule’s commissioner-nominees
Umar Muhammed, Lafia

The Nasarawa State House of Assembly has screened and confirmed all 15 commissioner-nominees sent to the House by Governor Abdullahi Sule.

The Speaker, Mr Ibrahim Abdullahi, announced this on Tuesday after completing the screening of six commissioner- nominees, who appeared for screening at the floor of the House in Lafia.

Abdullahi said, “If we will recall, on Monday, the House screened Ahmed Yahaya (Toto LGA), Philip Dada (Karu LGA), Othman Adam (Keffi LGA), Dr Abdulkarim Kana (Kokona LGA), Obadiah Boyi (Akwanga LGA), Yusuf Turaki (Awe LGA), Dr Salihu Alizaga (Nassarawa Eggon LGA), Dogo (Wamba LGA) and Prof. Otaki Allahnanah (Keana LGA).

“Today, we have screened the remaining six commissioner-nominees. They are, Haruna Adamu (Obi LGA), Ibrahim Ekye (Doma LGA), Mrs Fati Sabo (Nasarawa LGA), Abubakar Imam(Lafia LGA), Hajiya Halima Jabiru (Lafia LGA) and Mohammed Aliyu (Lafia LGA).

“The above screened nominees of Monday, October 21, and Tuesday, October 22, are hereby confirmed after the House finds them worthy of their appointments.”
