Four South Africa cops face trial for killing Nigerian
Adelani Adepegba, Abuja

Four South African policemen have been arrested for the murder of a Nigerian, Pius Abiaziem, in eastern cape on August 23.

The cops, it was gathered, would be arraigned in court for homicide immediately.

Chairman, Nigerians in the Diaspora Commission, Abike Dabiri-Erewa, confirmed the development on Thursday.
