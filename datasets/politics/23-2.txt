Kogi gets new Chief of Staff
Gbenga Odogun, Lokoja

Governor Yahaya Bello of Kogi State has appointed Abdulkareem Jamiu as his new Chief of Staff.

Jamiu, who was until now the Director-General of Protocol, replaces Edward David Onoja who is now the running mate to the governor.

Also, Dr Gabriel Ottah replaces AsukuJamiu as DG Protocol.
