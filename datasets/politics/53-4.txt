APC NWC appoints caretaker committee for Rivers
Kamarudeen Ogundele, Abuja

The National Working Committee of the All Progressives Congress has appointed a five-member executive caretaker committee for the Rivers State chapter of the party.

Members of the committee chaired by Isaac Ogbobula are Mr Friday Owhor, Mrs Beatrice Amobi and Prince Abolo Stephen, with Mr Baridon Badom as secretary.

A statement by the APC National Publicity Secretary, Lanre Issa-Onilu, said the committee would oversee the party’s affairs in the state.

According to the timetable of congresses approved by the NWC, ward congresses will hold on September 17 while appeals arising from ward congresses will hold between September 18 and 20.

The Local Government Area congresses will hold on September 21 while appeals arising from there will be heard between September 23 and 25.

“The state congress will hold on September 28 while appeals from that will be heard from September 30 to October 2,” the statement added.
