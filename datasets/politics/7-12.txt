Kogi election: Group endorses Adeyemi, Bello
Gbenga Odogun, Lokoja

A group, Coalition of Kogi West Senatorial Candidates, has endorsed Governor Yahaya Bello; his running mate, Chief Edward Onoja; and Senator Smart Adeyemi for the November 16 governorship and senatorial elections.

The candidates, who made this known at a joint press conference in Lokoja on Tuesday, said their decision was informed by the current demands of their party “and the need to alter a one direction political reality of Kogi State since 1992.”

The candidates were represented at the press conference by Olabode Adeyemi (African Democratic Congress), Roseline Ibitoye(Accord Party)Chief Samuel Atteh (PPA), Olasunkanmi Olayinka (CAP), Noah John Abiodun (PPN) and others.

Adeyemi, said, “We have resolved collectively to support the All Progressives Congress for the November 16 governorship and senatorial elections.”
