Kogi gov poll: Counting of votes begins at APC primary
Counting of votes has commenced at the All Progressives Congress primary for the November 16 Kogi governorship election, as voting and sorting ended, the News Agency of Nigeria reports.

A total of 3, 596 delegates out of 3,838, were accredited for the primary.
