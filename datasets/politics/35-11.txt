Taraba REC dies in Borno
Friday Olokor, Abuja

The Independent National Electoral Commission’s Resident Electoral Commissioner for Taraba State, Alhaji Baba Yusuf, is dead.

A statement on Sunday by INEC Secretary, Rose Oriarian-Anthony, said the REC died on Saturday at the University of Maiduguri Teaching Hospital in Borno State.

The statement read, “The Independent National Electoral Commission regrets to announce the death of the Resident Electoral Commissioner for Taraba State, Alhaji Baba Abba Yusuf.

“He died last (Saturday) night at the University of Maiduguri Teaching Hospital, Borno State, where he was on admission.”

He had since today, Sunday, September 29, 2019, been buried around 4pm in Maiduguri.

The commission was represented at the funeral by a delegation of the National Commissioners and Resident Electoral Commissioners.
