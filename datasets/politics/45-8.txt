Benue’ll conduct LG poll in November, says Ortom
John Charles, Makurdi

The Governor of Benue State, Samuel Ortom, on Tuesday, said the next local government election in the state would hold on November 30.

Ortom stated this during the inauguration of board members of the Local Government Commission at the new banquet hall of the Government House, Makurdi.

He said the Chairman of the State Independent Electoral Commission, Dr Joseph Loko, had informed him of the date .

He said, “The SIEC chairman had informed me that the commission has slated November 30 this year to conduct the local government election.

“The funding of the election will be borne by both the state and local governments.
