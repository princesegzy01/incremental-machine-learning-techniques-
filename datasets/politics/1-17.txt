Buhari, Pearson group meet in London
John Ameh, Abuja

President Muhammadu Buhari met with the management of Pearson Education Group in London on Wednesday.

The photograph of the meeting was shared by the Special Assistant to the President on New Media, Mr Bashir Ahmad, on his twitter handle, @BashirAhmad.

At the meeting were the Minister of Education, Mr Adamu Adamu; the Executive Secretary of the National Universities Commission, Prof. Abubakar Rasheed; and High Commissioner of Nigeria to the UK, Justice Adesola Oguntade.

The agenda of the meeting is unclear as of press time.

Buhari, who embarked on a private visit to London on November 2, will be away from Nigeria till November 17.
