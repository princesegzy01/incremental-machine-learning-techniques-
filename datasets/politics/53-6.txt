South-West records low crime rate – IGP
Peter Dada, Akure

The Inspector General of Police, Mr Abubakar Adamu, has declared that the South-West has lowest crime rate among the six geo-political zones of the Nigeria.

Adamu, who stated this on Thursday during his visit to the Ondo State Police Command, said the collaboration of the people at the community and police would go a long way to tackle all forms of criminality.

He said, “Statistics has shown that the South-West region records lowest crime rate in the country and I know it is as a result of the efforts of the police and members of the community.”

The IGP said the Federal Government had purchased amoured vehicles as part of the efforts to fight the criminals, particularly on the highways.
