INEC distributes PVCs in Bayelsa
Daniels Igoni, Yenagoa

The Independent National Electoral Commission has concluded arrangements to commence the distribution of 49,819 uncollected Permanent Voter Cards in Bayelsa State.

INEC’s Resident Electoral Commissioner in the state, Mr Monday Udoh, stated this on Tuesday during a stakeholders’ meeting on the modalities for the distribution of the PVCs by the commission.

According to the REC, who was represented by the commission’s Executive Secretary in the state, Mr Boco Ekong, the distribution of the PVCs would take place across the eight local government areas of the state.

He said the distribution was expected to start on September 2 and end on September 3.

He said, “The breakdown and the total number of PVCs to be distributed in the state is 49,819.”
