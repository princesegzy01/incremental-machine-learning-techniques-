Bayelsa APC begins gov poll campaign today
Daniels Igoni, Yenagoa

The All Progressives Congress in Bayelsa State has concluded arrangements to begin its campaigns for the forthcoming governorship election in the state.

To this end, the party has come up with a timetable for the campaign activities across the eight local government areas of the state.

The campaign itinerary was released in Yenagoa on Sunday by the Secretary, Media and Publicity Committee, APC Governorship Campaign Council, Mr Doifie Buokoribo.

It indicated that the campaigns would begin on Monday (today) with a visit to the Ogbia Local Government Area in Bayelsa East Senatorial District.

The other seven local government councils will take their turns subsequently from Tuesday at designated towns in the state.

According to the message, the APC will hold the grand finale on November 12 at the Ox-Bow Lake Pavillion in Yenagoa, the state capital.
