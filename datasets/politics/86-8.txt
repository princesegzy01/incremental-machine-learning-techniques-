FULL LIST: Chairmen, vice-chairmen of Reps standing committees, special panels
The Speaker of the House of Representatives, Femi Gbajabiamila, has named leaders of standing committees of the lower chamber of the National Assembly.

At the plenary on Thursday, the Speaker appointed chairmen and vice-chairmen of the committees.

Gbajabiamila also appointed several ad hoc committees and investigative panels.
