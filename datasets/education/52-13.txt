Gusau varsity gets new registrar
Adeniyi Olugbemi, Sokoto

The Federal University, Gusau, Zamfara State, has appointed Malam Yakubu Anivbassa as the new Registrar of the institution.

Anivbassa was appointed after interviews conducted for applicants by the university’s Governing Council during its 12th meeting held on December 10, 2018.

The appointment was confirmed in a letter issued to Anivbassa on January 8, 2019, and signed by the Acting Registrar, I.U Sauwa.

Anivbassa, a graduate of Mass Communication from the University of Nigeria, Nsukka, obtained his Post-Graduate Diploma in Public Administration and a Master of Public Administration from Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria, in 2002 and 2009, respectively.

He hails from Okene Local Government Area of Kogi State, and until his appointment as the second substantive registrar of the Federal University Gusau, he was a Deputy Registrar in the Institute of Development Research and Training, Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria.

In his 24 years of service with Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria, Anivbassa served as an administrative officer in 1998, Assistant Registrar in 2001, Senior Assistant Registrar in 2004, Principal Assistant Registrar in 2007 and Deputy Registrar in 2011.

Anivbassa’s appointment is for a single term of five years.
