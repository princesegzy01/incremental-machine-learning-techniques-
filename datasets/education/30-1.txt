Culture is significant in early childhood education, says Adenmosun
Dayo Ojerinde

The Chairperson, World Organisation for Early Childhood Education in Lagos State, Mrs Olusola Adenmosun, has said the organisation is ready to improve the quality of early childhood education by focusing on areas that will thoroughly impact knowledge in the child.

Speaking during an event aimed at celebrating Children’s Day held at the University of Lagos, Adenmosun expressed the organisation’s commitment to childhood education, saying there was a need to further raise awareness of childhood education and promote mutual understanding among children.

She added that the organisation’s aim was to promote the optimal condition for all children and ensure their well-being, development, and happiness within their family unit and in the communities they live.
