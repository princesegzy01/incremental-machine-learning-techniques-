BIU alumni awards scholarship to post graduate student
Sola Shittu, Benin

An alumnus of Benson Idahosa university, BIU, has offered a fully funded scholarship for master degree to the best overall student in the university who is interested in furthering his or her education at Benson Idahosa university.

The Vice-Chancellor of BIU, Prof Sam Guobadia who disclosed this at the fifteenth convocation ceremony of the university said the donor of the award was the best graduating student of the class of 2008 from the accounting department of the university.

He said an alumnus has also pledged the sum of one million naira for the over all best graduating student effective from 2020 convocation ceremony.

Guobadia, while commending the laudable move, charged the 2019 “class of elite” to be challenged by the alumni and also plan to give back when they are blessed.

Out of the 741 graduating students this year, 593 graduated with baccalaureate and 148 post graduate degrees.

The list of graduates also include 43 first class, 190 second Class upper division, 330 second class lower division and 30 third class division.
