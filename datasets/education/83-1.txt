Wesley varsity gets new VC
The Methodist Church of Nigeria has approved and confirmed the appointment of Prof Sunday Ukachukwu as the second substantive Vice-Chancellor of Wesley University, Ondo State, effective July 1, 2018.

Ukachukwu is a professor of Animal Science at the Michael Okpara University of Agriculture, Umudike in Abia State.

“The church, through the university’s governing council, appreciated the acting Vice-Chancellor, Prof Williams Olu-Aderounmu, for his contribution to the development of the university during his short tenure,” a statement from the university read.
