Ondo AAC chair elected to lead Akungba varsity’s alumni
Dayo Ojerinde

The Ondo State Chairman of the Africa Action Congress, Mr Alex Adeniyi, has been elected the new Chairman of the Adekunle Ajasin University Akungba Akoko Alumni Association, Akure Chapter.

Adeniyi was elected unopposed at the Monthly meeting of the Association held in Akure on Saturday.

He is to be assisted by Ayodeji Oladimeji, (Vice Chairman), Rasak Babajide, (Secretary), David Akinnadeju, (Fin. Secretary), Esther Aladetimehin (Treasurer) and Eniola Samson as Public Relations Officer of the association.

In his response, Adeniyi thanked his fellow alumnus for the confidence they have in him, he promised to lead the association to greater heights.

“Today is another milestone in the history of our association. The task of moving to the next level is a collective responsibility which is why I seek the support of all and Sundry to make a success of our administration,” Adeniyi said.
