FETHA now AE-FUNAI teaching hospital, says FG
Edward Nnachi, Abakaliki

The Federal Teaching Hospital, Abakaliki, has been made a teaching hospital by the Federal Government for the MBBS programme of Alex Ekwueme Federal University, Ndufu-Alike Ikwo, Ebonyi State.

This was contained in a statement made available to journalists on Saturday in Abakaliki by the Public Relations Officer of the university, Mr Iyke Ubochi.

Ubochi, who quoted the Vice-Chancellor of the university, Prof. Chinedum Nwajiuba, said the change was made known during the 53rd meeting of the Senate held on Friday.

According to the PRO, following this development, the Federal Teaching Hospital, Abakaliki, will be renamed Alex Ekwueme Federal University Teaching Hospital.

The statement read, “Therefore, FETHA will now be referred to as Alex Ekwueme Federal University Teaching Hospital. The good news was made known by the Vice-Chancellor of the university during the 53rd meeting of Senate held on Friday, 14th February 2019.”
