JABU partners ICAN on MCATI for students
The Joseph Ayo Babalola University has signed a Memorandum of Understanding with the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria under Mutual Cooperation Agreement with Tertiary Institutions to ensure the professionalism of accounting practice in Nigeria.

Speaking at the official signing ceremony of the MoU, ICAN President, Alhaji Razak Jaiyeola, in his speech, said the MoU was essentially between ICAN and three universities, namely the Joseph Ayo Babalola University, Ikeji Arakeji; Osun State University, Oshogbo and Pan Atlantic University, Lagos.

According to Mallam Isma’lla Zakari, “The focus of the MoU is to integrate ICAN syllabus into JABU’s curriculum for the ICAN qualifying professional examination”.

Present at the signing of the Memorandum of Understanding with the Institute were the Acting Vice-Chancellor, Prof. T.O. Olowokure; Deputy Registrar, Mr. J. O. Babalola; Head of Department, Accounting Department, Mr. Akindele Akosile; and Dean, College of Law, Dr. Ola Jejelola.
