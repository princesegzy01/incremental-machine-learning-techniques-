Edo teachers to celebrate Obaseki’s award with rally
Teachers in Edo State have concluded plans to hold a mega rally to celebrate Governor Godwin Obaseki’s emergence as the Nigeria Union of Teachers’ Best Performing Governor for 2019 and his transformation of the state’s basic education sector.

In a letter to the governor, NUT National Secretary-General, Dr. Mike Ene, said Governor Obaseki would be bestowed with the Best Performing Governor on Saturday, October 5, 2019, based on his efforts in improving Basic Education and teachers’ welfare in the state.

The state Commissioner of Education, Hon. Jimoh Ijegbai, directed all teachers in the state to assemble on Friday 4, October, 2019 at the Samuel Ogbemudia Secondary School (New Era) Upper Mission Road, Benin City for a visit to the governor.
