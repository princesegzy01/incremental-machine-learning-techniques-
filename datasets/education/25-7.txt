Eco-friendly environment increases lifespan – Mejabi
Dayo Ojerinde

The Principal of Linsey High School, Lekki, Lagos, Mr David Mejabi, has called on Nigerians to embrace eco-friendly technologies, stating that it increases lifespan.

Mejabi said this on Thursday in Lagos during the school’s eco-friendly science fair showcase.

“To eradicate environmental pollution and increase our lifespan, we must look into getting an eco-friendly environment. The rate of pollution in the country is alarming and it has sent thousands of species into extinction,” he said.

Mejabi advised the government to invest more in renewable energy, “When we engage in renewable energy, it will help to sustain our environment for the future generation. That is what the pupils of Linsey want to achieve.”

The science fair coordinator of the school, Mr Sunday Chukwu, described an eco-friendly environment as a panacea for development.

No fewer than 21 gadgets produced by the pupils of the school were displayed during the fair.
