Ado poly gets new registrar, bursar
Abiodun Nejo, Ado Ekiti

The management of the Federal Polytechnic, Ado-Ekiti, in Ekiti State, has approved the appointment of Mrs Kehinde Ekanem as Registrar and Mr John Omodara as Bursar.

Ekanem, until her appointment, was a Deputy Registrar in the institution, while Omodara, until his appointment, was a Deputy Bursar at the National Open University of Nigeria, Abuja.

This was made known in a statement by the Deputy Registrar, Information, Ade Adejolu, which said that “the appointment of the registrar and bursar formed part of the highpoints of a recent meeting of the Governing Council, which upheld results of the interview conducted recently for the two vacant positions in the polytechnic.”

The Rector of the institution, Dr Dayo Oladebeye, advised the two appointees to see the appointments as a call to service.

Oladebeye congratulated the duo, while inaugurating them at an emergency meeting of management of the institution, and welcomed them to the polytechnic’s management committee.

Ekanem holds a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Languages from the University of Calabar, obtained in 1995 and a Masters of Public Administration Degree from the Ekiti State University, Ado-Ekiti in 2010.

Omodara, who holds a Higher National Diploma in Accountancy from the Federal Polytechnic, Ado-Ekiti in 1995 also has a Post Graduate Diploma in Financial Management in 2002 and as well as a Master of Business Administration from the Ekiti State University in 2005.
