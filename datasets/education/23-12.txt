Bauchi Poly matriculates 6,983 students, commences more courses
Armstrong Bakam, Bauchi

The Federal Polytechnic, Bauchi has matriculated 6, 983 students for the 2018/2019 academic session.

This is even as the polytechnic is set to commence 10 degree courses. The rector of the institution, Sunusi Gumau, disclosed this on Saturday during the matriculation ceremony of the newly admitted students of the polytechnic.

He explained that the polytechnic received 7, 322 applications but 6, 983 applicants were admitted. He gave the breakdown of the classes of the registered new intakes as: diploma, 297, National Diploma 2, 448, Higher National Diploma 2, 046, IJMB, 28, Pre-ND, 61 and remedial, 7.

Gumau said the programmess to be commenced are in affiliation with Abubakar Tafawa Balewa University, Bauchi.

He said the programmes to be introduced are degrees in agriculture and bio-resources engineering, civil engineering, electrical engineering, mechanical engineering, computer science, architecture, building technology, estate management and valuation, quantity surveying and survey and geo-informatics.

The rector said the polytechnic had recently received a high-powered delegation from the National Universities Commission who visited the institution for resource verification for all their engineering and environmental programs.

He said, “This is in addition to the efforts we are making to run postgraduate diploma programmes in the polytechnic. About one month ago, we were at the Nigerian Defence Academy, Kaduna for the signing of a Memorandum of Understanding for PGD in our engineering programs. This also we shall commence soon.”
