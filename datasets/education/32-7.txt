AAUA Lagos alumni gets new executives
Dayo Ojerinde

The Lagos chapter of the Alumni Association of AdekunleAjasin University, Akungba-Akoko, Ondo State, has elected new executives to lead the association for the next two years.

The election, which was held on Monday in Lagos, saw Mr Samuel Akinrimisi emerge as the Chairman; Mr Babtunde Afolabi elected as Vice-Chairman; Mr Richard Okpa as Secretary; Chinelo Okeke as Treasurer, while Folakemi Akinsuyi, emerged Financial Secretary.

After the election, Akinrimisi called for the cooperation of members of the association to reposition the chapter to be service-oriented. “We are poised to create enduring values that will not only guide us, but also outlive us. Emphatically, the Lagos chapter should be seen as a home for all, where we all shall put hands on deck to make it habitable and service-oriented. We shall work assiduously with team spirit, integrity, transparency, accountability and sense of urgency.

“With our vision, Connecting for greater exploits, this administration is determined to set the pace for the alumni worldwide, as such, we are aimed at creating a homely and resourceful platform for all members, as well as building an enduring database for the chapter,” Akinrinmisi said.
