Varsity administrators hold conference
The Association of University Professional Administrators has said that it is ready to hold its 2018 National Conference and Annual General Assembly, with the theme, ‘Embracing the Future: Creativity and Innovation in University Management,’ on December 5 at the Delta State University, Abraka.

Disclosing this in a statement issued on Monday the Public Relations Officer of the association, Mr Nath Tyoaan, said the conference also aimed at calling for the application of effective and positive corporate governance principles to move the country’s university system forward.

He said it was about time the country’s university system responded to the demands of future growth and development by embracing new management techniques emerging from best academic, as well as professional practices all over the world.
