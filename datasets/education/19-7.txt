Ekiti school seeks parents’ cooperation
Abiodun Nejo, Ado Ekiti

The Head Teacher, Michealian Catholic Nursery and Primary School, Ado Ekiti, Rev. Sister Veronica Babalola, has canvassed the cooperation of parents to ensure their wards excel in their studies.

Babalola, who said the cooperation among pupils, teachers and parents was responsible for the school’s outstanding academic performance, urged parents across Nigeria to take a cue from the school.

The head teacher, who spoke in Ado Ekiti during the end of the year, graduation and prizes giving ceremony, said, “The school is known for moral teaching and academic excellence, as well beautiful and conducive environment”.

She charged the graduating pupils, many of who received prizes on account of their academic performances, high morals and neatness, to be good ambassadors of the school.

The Alasa of Ilasa- Ekiti, Oba Omolagba Ajayi, said, “With the beautiful environment, the pupils here would be beautiful in and out and learning will be enhanced.”

Oba Ajayi, who was represented by his wife, Olori Anike Ajayi; the Ajore of Ilasa Ekiti, High Chief L.O. Ajayi; and the Odofin of Ilasa Ekiti, High Chief M.O. Ojerinde, urged teachers not to relent in their efforts at ensuring quality education delivery.
