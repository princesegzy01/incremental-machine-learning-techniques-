UKEAS Nigeria holds overseas study tour
The United Kingdom Education Advisory Services Nigeria will hold a study tour for intending students and applicants to different international universities and provide them with free consultation, free study visa application amongst other slated activities.

The tour will see the UKEAS Nigeria team making stops in five cities across Nigeria, including Ibadan, Enugu, Port Harcourt, Kano and Jos. During this study tour, intending students and applicants will get an opportunity to meet and interact with representatives of over 27 different universities from the United Kingdom, United States of America, Canada and Ireland. Attendees will also get access to on-the-spot admissions, study visa counselling, bank compliance checks, advice on credibility interviews and sponsorship documents, as well as scholarships information.

The UKEAS is an overseas education consultancy that provides free and impartial counselling and application services for students wishing to study and live abroad. Providing services that are thorough, and completely free, the agency ensures that prospective students have an easy and straightforward overseas study application process.
