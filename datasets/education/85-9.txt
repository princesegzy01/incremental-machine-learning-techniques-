Adebule commissions Grande OakBridge’s permanent site
Deputy Governor of Lagos State, Dr. Idiat Adebule, has commissioned the permanent site of Grande OakBridge Montessori School, Osapa London, Lagos.

Represented by the Director, Office of Quality Assurance, Ministry of Education, Mrs. Ronke Soyombo, Adebule lauded the school’s ability to take the curriculum and turn it into something great for the pupils.

Her words, “For more than a decade, the school has kept the vision of developing children equitably, thus complementing the effort of the government. This site will no doubt provide an improved environment and culture for learning.”

The proprietress of the school, Mrs. Royoke Obalade, said, “Thirteen years after establishment, we have moved to our permanent site. For achieving this feat, we appreciate past and present parents for entrusting their children to us. We thank the staff and board for their unflinching support. Above all, we give glory to God. This school was founded to raise children that are academically and morally sound and we will never compromise on quality.”

Speaking through his wife, Mrs. Morenike Okoh, the chairman on the occasion, Mr. Alex Okoh, who is a Member of the Presidential Economic Advisory Council, said, “I congratulate the board and proprietor for expanding the OakBridge vision to what we see today. It is a move to improve the environment for further delivery of quality service.”
