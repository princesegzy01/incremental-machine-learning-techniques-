Two Offa poly students bag FG scholarships
Tunde Oyekola

Two students of the Federal Polytechnic Offa, Kwara State, have been offered scholarship for the 2019/2020 Federal Government Scholarship for Students in Nigeria Public Tertiary Institutions.

The beneficiaries, according to a statement by the Head, Public Relations and Protocols of the institution, Olayinka Iroye, are Jaiyeola Sofiat, an ND 2, Accountancy student and Olagoke Williams an HND 1, Mechanical Engineering student.

The award letters signed by the Director, Federal Scholarship Board, on behalf of the Ministry of Education stated that each scholarship was for two years and at N100,000 per session.

The Rector of the institution, Dr Lateef Olatunji, congratulated the beneficiaries and charged them to continue in their commendable academic performances as such could win them more valued scholarship opportunities in future.

He further implored other students to be serious with their studies and be confident enough to apply for scholarships whenever it was available.

Olatunji commended the Federal Government for coming up with a series of programmes designed towards eradicating illiteracy in the country and urged it to avail the underprivileged more access to quality education.

The beneficiaries appreciated Muhammed Buhari administration for coming up with programmes that would make academic activities flourish in the country.

They also thanked the polytechnic management for creating an enabling environment for students of the polytechnic to benefit from the scholarship.
