Eze’s brace helps move QPR to fifth
Kelvin Ekerete

Super Eagles target, Eberechi Eze, scored a brace as Queen Park Rangers recorded their seventh win of the season in a 3-2 win at Hull City on Saturday in the championship.

Jarrod Bowen opened the scoring for the home side in the 29th minute while Ryan Manning scored the equalizer in the 44th minute.

The second half belonged to Eze who scored in the 78th minute to give the R’s the lead before scoring his brace 10 minutes later in the 88th minute. Hull City pulled one back in the 90th minute as QPR held on for the win which moved them to fifth on the table.

Eze has scored in 11 league appearances for the R which has made a person of interest to the Nigerian national team.
