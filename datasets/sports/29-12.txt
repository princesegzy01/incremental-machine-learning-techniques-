NWPL Super four: Rivers Angels, Confluence Queens through to final
Kelvin Ekerete

Rivers Angels will face Confluence Queens in the final of the Nigeria Women Premier League Super 4 tournament in Agege on Sunday to determine the champions of the 2019 league season.

Rivers Angels defeated defending league champions Bayelsa Queens 4-2 on penalties, after regulation time ended goalless while Confluence Queen, making their first appearance in the Super Four competition since 2012 were 2-0 winners over Adamawa Queens.

Arit Usang scored a brace in the third and 26th minute to hand the Kogi side a ticket to the final where they will play Rivers Angels.

The third place match will also be played on Sunday at the same venue.

The champions of this season’s Nigeria Women’s Premier League will be rewarded with three million naira and a week sponsored tour to Spain by Spanish league top-flight organisers, La Liga.
