Eze named QPR Player of The Month
Kelvin Ekerete

Super Eagles target, Eberechi Eze, has been named Queen Park Rangers’ Player of The Month for October.

Eze scored three goals also providing two assists in five games for the R’s as they won twice with two losses and one draw to sit eight on the league table.

Eze has been in blistering form for QPR this season with his performance catching the attention of Premier League sides Tottenham Hotspurs and Southampton earlier in the season.

The 21-year old forward has scored six goals in 14 appearances for the R’s in the Championships. Eze is eligible to play for Nigeria through his parents.

Fans of the club had also called for the forward to be named Player of The Month for the Championships after his impressive display.

Lukas Ross replying to the post on Instagram wrote, “Well deserved too, now time for some EFL recognition.”

Ikidd.4 wrote, “Should be championship player of The Month as well.”
