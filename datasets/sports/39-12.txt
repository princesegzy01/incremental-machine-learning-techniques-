Wike ratifies agreement on Madrid Academy
Chukwudi Akasike, Port Harcourt

The Rivers State Government and Real Madrid have signed a ratification agreement for the development of Real Madrid Academy, Port Harcourt, into an international football academy.

The event took place on Monday at the Santiago Bernabeu Stadium, home of Spanish giants Madrid.

A Government House statement signed by the Special Assistant to the Rivers State Governor, Electronic Media, Mr. Simeon Nwakaudu, indicated that the signature of the ratification protocol was carried out by Governor Nyesom Wike and the Vice President of Real Madrid Foundation, Enrique Sanchez.

In an address, Wike said the state government was preparing the Real Madrid Academy as a veritable pathway for the empowerment of Rivers children.

“We are preparing the Real Madrid Academy as a veritable pathway for our young stars to be international football talents and for them to realise their dreams.

“We will offer them exposure, nurturing opportunities and mentorship by world renowned professionals at the Real Madrid Academy.” The governor said.

He told Madrid’s management that the Rivers State Government had already exhibited seriousness by developing the needed infrastructure at the academy in Port Harcourt.

Sanchez commended the state government for its commitment to using football as a tool to empower youths.

He assured the government that the Madrid foundation would continue to partner with it for the training and exposure of Rivers children.
