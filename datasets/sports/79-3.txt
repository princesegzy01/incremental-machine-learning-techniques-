Brazil vs Nigeria: Key battles
Ebenezer Bajela

This might be a friendly game but both teams are eager for the best result in Singapore when they take on to the pitch on Sunday (today).

Considering the talents in both teams, many would tip the Selecao to come out victorious but Gernot Rohr’s men will definitely not go down without a fight following key additions to the team.

Sports Extra takes a look at where the battle could be won and lost.

Midfield

The South Americans looks to have an edge over the Nigerians in this area, but the Eagles can also boast of quality players here.

Both teams like to hold on to the ball and pass it around, but the question is, who will do it better? Whoever wins this battle may most likely win the day. It promises to be a battle to the finish with the likes of Barcelona’s Authur, Madrid’s Casemiro, Liverpool’s Fabinho and Bayern’s Coutinho contesting against Eagles midfielders Wilfred Ndidi, Joe Aribo, Ramon Azeez and Alex Iwobi.
