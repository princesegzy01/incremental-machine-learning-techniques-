Leganes will bounce back after Madrid thrashing – Awaziem
Kelvin Ekerete

After their 5-0 thrashing in the hands of Real Madrid on Wednesday in the Spanish La Liga, Leganes defender, Chidozie Awaziem, has promised fans that the team will bounce back in their next league game on Sunday when they welcome Eibar to the Estadio Municipal de Butarque.

The Madrid side recorded their first win of the current league season against Real Mallorca a week ago but were out played when they faced second-placed Madrid at the Santiago Bernabéu Stadium.

Reacting to the game’s outcome, the Nigerian international stated that the result was not what they expected but has promised the team will get back their rhythm on Sunday while also calling on the fans to keep supporting the team.

“This wasn’t the result we wanted but I am sure we will bounce back and get pass these hard times. Thanks to our fans who always show their support and encouragement, together we will achieve our aim; we need you all on Sunday,” he wrote on Instagram.

The 22-year-old who is currently on loan to the Spanish outfit from FC Porto till the end of the season has been capped seven times in the La Liga and played for the entire duration of the game.
