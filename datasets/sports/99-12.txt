2019 NBBF League tips off October 13
Johnny Edward

The 2019 Nigeria Basketball Federations men’s Premier League season will tip-off from October 13 to November 30.

The men’s league will see teams divided into the Atlantic and Savannah conferences for a round-robin league format.

The first phase of the Atlantic Conference will hold in Akure, while Savannah teams will take place in the Indoor Sports Hall of the Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria between October 12 and 22.

The second phase will take place from November 2 to 12 with all points scored in the first phase carried over.

The Savannah Conference train will move to Package B of the Moshood Abiola Stadium, Abuja, while Atlantic Conference teams will battle for supremacy at the Indoor Sports Hall of the Ilorin Township stadium.

The top four teams in each conference will qualify for the National Final 8 from November 24 to 30.

Oluyole Warriors, Customs, Rivers Hoopers, Kwara Falcons, Hoops and Read, Delta Force, Police Baton, and Raptors are some of the teams that will be competing for honours in the Atlantic Conference.

Others include Dodan Warriors, Islanders, who hope to rekindle their rivalry alongside newly promoted Invaders, Coal City and Akure Raiders.
