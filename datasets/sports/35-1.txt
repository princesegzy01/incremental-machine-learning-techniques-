2019/20 NPFL: Alimi rearing to go for Lobi
Solape Lawal-Solarin

Lobi Star striker, Sikiru Alimi, is bracing up for the new Nigerian Professional Football League season which will kick off on November 3.

The Markurdi based outfit will face Plateau United away in their opening league fixture.

“I am just gearing up for the new season.

“I want to do well with Lobi in the league. We are keen on doing well in the league, hopefully, things will work out well for us,” he told Sports Extra in a telephone conversation.

Alimi was part of the Eagles B team that lost 4-3 on aggregate to Togo in a African Nation Championship qualifier .
