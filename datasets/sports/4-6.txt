Moses to give Nantes fans jerseys
Ebenezer Bajela

Super Eagles and Nantes forward, Simon Moses is set to give fans his signed jerseys following his imperious form last month.

Moses was voted Nantes Player of The Month for October after putting up some impressive displays, scoring four goals, including a hat-trick against FC Paris in the French Coup de la Ligue.

And the club announced on its website that fans will be given signed jerseys of the Nigeria international.

“Elected Canari of the Month by Iliane for the month of October, Simon Moses offers subscribers to win his signed jersey,” the club wrote on its website.

Moses has scored a total of five goals in 12 matches this season.
