NPFL: Lobi record biggest win, MFM stage comeback
Sodiq Oyeleke

Lobi Stars recorded the biggest win on match day two of the 2019/20 Nigeria Professional Football League, thrashing Delta Force 4-0 at the Aper Aku Stadium, Makurdi.

In the Sunday’s game, Samuel Tiza, Sikiru Alimi, Abdulkareem Mumini and Douglas Achiv scored for the home team.

MFM recovered from a goal down to draw 1-1 against visiting FC IfeanyiUbah at the Agege Stadium Lagos.

Christian Nnaji’s sixth minute opener for IfeanyiUbah was cancelled out by MFM’s Adeniji Kabir in the 37th minute.

A close range shot by Sixtus Forsah secured a shock 1-0 win for Wikki Tourists against Nasarawa United at the Lafia Township Stadium on Sunday.

Sunshine Stars drew visiting Warri Wolves 1-1 at the Ondo Sports Complex, Akure.

Plateau United registered a 1-0 away win against Jigawa Golden Stars while Enyimba forced Katsina United to a 1-1 draw at the Muhammadu Dikko Stadium.

In Port Harcourt, Rivers United secure a 1-1 home draw against Heartland.
