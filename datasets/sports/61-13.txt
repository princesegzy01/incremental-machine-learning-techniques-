Sirika leads polo team to Georgian Cup win
Adelani Adepegba, Abuja

The Abuja Robicom team led by the Minister of Aviation, Senator Hadi Sirika, has won the centenary polo tournament in Kaduna.

The champions were presented the famous Georgian Cup at a ceremony witnessed by Governor Aminu Masari of Katsina State and the Comptroller-General of Nigeria Immigration Service, Muhammad Babandede, on Saturday.

The game was also witnessed by the Emir of Katsina, Alh. Muhammad Usman, and a former Military Administrator, Col. Abdu Mohammed (retd.)

Babandede congratulated the NIS polo team captained by Musa Hamza, for making the service proud.

He also donated a trophy to the Most Valuable Player at the tournament, Hamisu Buba.

The NIS spokesman, DCI James Sunday, in a statement said many valuable local and foreign polo players participated in the tourney.
