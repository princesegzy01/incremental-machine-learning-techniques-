Mikel doubtful for Gaziantep clash
Johnny Edward

Mikel Obi is a major doubt for Trabzonspor’s Turkish Super Lig home clash against Gaziantep on Saturday, Sports Extra reports.

Obi suffered a thigh injury in training on Monday and did not complete the session.

He also missed his club’s training session on Tuesday at the Mehmet Ali Yilmaz Facilities according to a report on Turkish daily, Fanatik.

Mikel has made six league appearances for Trabzonspor this season.

Trabzonspor are currently third in the Turkish Super Lig table with 12 points from seven games.
