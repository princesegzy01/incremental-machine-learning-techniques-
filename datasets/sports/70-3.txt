CHAN: Imama names 20-man squad for Togo battle
Coach Imama Amapakabo has unveiled a list of 20 senior home-based players for the second leg of the 2020 African Nations Championship qualifiers against Togo in Lagos on Saturday.

In a statement by the Nigeria Football Federation on Thursday, Super Eagles goalkeeper tops the list of players invited for the crucial tie.

Others are Nasarawa United forward Sunusi Ibrahim and Akwa United’s Ndifreke Effiong, who netted a brace for the U-23 team in the 5-0 defeat of Sudan in Asaba in September.

Kano Pillars Nyima Nwagua, Fatai Gbadamosi of Shooting Stars and Lobi Stars’ trio of Ebube Duru, Sikiru Alimi and John Lazarus also make the list, alongside goalkeeper Adamu Abubakar of Wikki Tourists and Enugu Rangers’ Anthony Shimaga.

Nigeria will hope to overturn the 4-1 defeat they suffered in the hands of the Sparrow Hawks in the first leg in Lome
