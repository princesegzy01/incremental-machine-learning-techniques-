Team Nigeria crash out of men, women’s relays
Kelvin Ekerete

Nigeria’s women and men’s 4x100m relay teams failed to make the final of the relay event after finishing seventh while the men’s team were disqualified.

The quartet of Enoch Adegoke, Usheoritse Itsekiri, Ogho-Oghene Egwero and Seye Ogunlewe ran in lane 8 and finished last in in 38.62sec but were later disqualified.

In the women’s category, Joy Udo-Gabriel took the lead-off leg while Blessing Okagbare ran the second leg Mercy Ntia Obong ran the third leg while Rosemary Chukwuma anchored for the team as they finished seventh in the race with a SB of 43.05s.

South Africa will represent Africa in the final of the men’s 4x100m relay after finishing first in the men’s relay with a time of 37.65sec with the Asaba 2018 100m gold medalist anchoring.
