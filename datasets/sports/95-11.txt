Apochie wins ninth career fight
Kelvin Ekerete

Nigerian boxer, Efetobor Apochie, defeated United States fighter Larry Pryor to record his ninth career win on Saturday in the cruiserweight division at the Arena Theatre, Houston, Texas.

Apochie, who turned professional in 2017, has won all his nine fights through knockouts, and it took him four rounds to knock out Pryor, before the fight was stopped.

Reacting to the win, Apochie took to his Facebook account to appreciate the fans for their continued support over the years.

“For it is done unto every man as he believes. Thanks to everyone for the support, I appreciate you guys a lot. Mostly @futureofboxing @twincharlo and all my Nigeria fans love you guys and I wish you all more blessing.”

Apochie’s compatriot, Efe Ajagba, is expected to take to the ring for his 11th career fight on October 26.
