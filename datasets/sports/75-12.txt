Team South-West win 2019 Wemalympics in Lagos
Wema Bank’s South -West region has emerged winners of the 2019 Wemalympics held at the University of Lagos Sports Complex.

During the sporting competition which held at weekend, Wema Bank’s staff expressed excitement and commended the bank’s management for putting the event together.

Events held in the competition are scrabble, sack race, caterpillar race, egg race, arm wrestling, tug of war, match pass, football, as well as electronic games.

Team South-West came first after accumulating 20 medals: 11 gold, six silver and three bronze.

Team South-South were second with 20 medals: three gold, 10 silver, and seven bronze, while Team North were third with 15 medals – six gold, three silver and six bronze.

Team Lagos finished fourth with 18 medals – four gold, five silver, and nine bronze.

The Managing Director/Chief Executive Officer of Wema Bank, Ademola Adebise, said the competition, which is in its third year, was organised to foster collaboration among the bank’s members of staff.

Also, Wema Bank’s Deputy Managing Director, Moruf Oseni, urged members of staff to extend the lessons learnt from the sporting event to their workplace.
