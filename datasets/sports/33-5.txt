India Cup: Top stars light up final
Notable low handicap player and former India Cup champion, Tim Ayomike as well as Dapo Akande, Akeem Lawal, Hetal Zahl and high-flying Peter Eben-Spiff are some of the golfers expected to light up the field on Saturday in the final of the India Cup at the Ikoyi Club 1938, Lagos.

Earlier in the week, the tournament produced champions among the caddies and staff of the club. Sola Ogunoye, with 80-gross score, won in the caddies’ event, while Favor Efenedo, with 94-gross score, won in the ladies’ category in the caddies’ 18-hole round.

Benjamin Emili was top in the staff category.

However, Saturday’s clash among the big hitters would decide where the tournament trophy would head.

Chairman of the tournament’s Organising Committee, Sunil Thadani, said the said they were happy with the success recorded so far.

He said he expected a tough tournament on Saturday, noting that the interest shown in the competition had been very encouraging.
