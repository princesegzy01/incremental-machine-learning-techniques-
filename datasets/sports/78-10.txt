Teenage star Coco Gauff wins first WTA title
Coco Gauff won her first WTA title on Sunday following a 6-3, 1-6, 6-2 final win over Jelena Ostapenko that saw her take the trophy aged 15.

Gauff completed a dream week in Linz to became the youngest player to win a WTA event since Czech Nicole Vaidisova triumphed in Vancouver in 2004.
