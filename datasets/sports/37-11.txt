LSSC: Tandoh out, Aiyepeku in
Sodiq Oyeleke

Olusola Aiyepeku has replaced Kweku Tandoh as the Executive Chairman for the Lagos State Sports Commission Governing Board.

His appointment was announced by the LSSC Director of Public Relations, Titi Oshodi, on Tuesday.

According to the statement, Aiyepeku’s appointment took effect on Monday.

Before Aiyepeku’s appointment, Tandoh headed the commission since 2017.

“His appointment is contained in a letter dated October 27, 2019. Aiyepeku’s appointment takes effect from the October 28, 2019 for a four-year term,” Oshodi added.

The PUNCH gathered that upon his resumption into office, Aiyepeku summoned a meeting with the management staff at the sports commission’s office inside Teslim Balogun Stadium in Surulere.

During the meeting, he assured members of staff of the commission that he was committed to rejuvenating sports in Lagos State.

“My focus is on one goal, getting sports in Lagos to be number one in Nigeria,’’ he said.

Until his appointment, he was the acting Chief Executive Officer of Kinetic Sports Management, organisers of Copa Lagos Beach Soccer event.

He served as Special Assistant to the late chairman of LSSC, Deji Tinubu.

Aiyepeku is a Chemical Engineering graduate of University of Lagos with a banking background and deep-rooted passion for sports.
