Etebo thanks fans for Stoke win
Ebenezer Bajela

Super Eagles midfielder, Oghenekaro Etebo, thanked Stoke City fans for their support in their 2-0 win over Fulham on Saturday.

Etebo, who played for 90 minutes, thanked the fans as they claimed a second straight victory in the Championship, having gone eight games without a win.

“Another three points.Well done lads and thanks to all the Amazing fans of city for their support,” he wrote on Twitter.

The victory moves Stoke away from the bottom of the table to 23 on the log.
