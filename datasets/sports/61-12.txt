Unilorin win JAMB football tourney
Sodiq Oyeleke

University of Ilorin defeated Nuhu Bamali Polytechnic of Zaria 1-0 to win the third edition of the Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board-sponsored tertiary institutions football competition.

Nuhu Bamali Polytechnic went home with N750,000 as runners-up.
