Levante, Newcastle celebrate Martins at 35
’Tosin Oluwalowo

Former Super Eagles striker Obafemi Martins has received well wishes on the celebration of his 35th birthday.

Among those who sent messages to the striker on social media are La Liga, Levante, Newcastle and the Nigeria Football Federation.

Some of his ex-teammates were also not left out in wishing him a happy birthday.

Martins played for Newcastle between 2006 and 2009, scoring 28 goals in 88 appearances.

He also spent 18 months at Levante between September 2012 and March 2013, scoring seven goals in 21 games, before moving to the Major League Soccer in the US.

“On this day seven years ago a Nigerian striker celebrated his birthday in style scoring a brace for Levante. Happy 35th Obafemi Martins,” La Liga wrote on Twitter, accompanied by the video of the goals.

“Happy birthday, Obafemi Martins,” Newcastle wrote.

“Happy birthday Obafemi Martins! This is our present to you. Cheers mate,” Levante also wrote.

“Happy birthday to the one and only Obafemi Martins,” The NFF tweeted.

Martins, who is well known for his somersaults scored 19 times in 42 international caps for Nigeria.
