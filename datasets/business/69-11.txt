UPDC seeks to raise N15.96bn capital
Feyisayo Popoola

UACN Property Development Company Plc has revealed plans to raise N15.96bn capital through a rights issue.

The Nigerian Stock Exchange said in a statement on Monday that the UPDC had through its stockbroker, Stanbic IBTC Stockbrokers Limited submitted an application for the approval and listing of a rights issue of 15,961,563,260 ordinary shares of 50 kobo each at N1 per share.

It said the rights issue was on the basis of 43 new ordinary shares for every seven ordinary shares held.

The NSE said the qualification date for the rights was Monday, September 30, 2019.

The UPDC, whose current outstanding shares stand at 2,598,395,791, would have a total of 18,559,959,051 shares if the rights issue records 100 per cent success.
