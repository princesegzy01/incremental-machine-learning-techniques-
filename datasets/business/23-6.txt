FCMB expands branch network
Nike Popoola

First City Monument Bank says it has opened an ultra-modern and full-service branch along Agege Motor Road in Oshodi, Lagos State.

A statement said with this development, customers of the FCMB around the axis of Ladipo in Mushin now had another convenient location for their transactions to enjoy the valued-added offerings which the bank had been known for since its establishment 36 years ago.

It stated, “The opening of the new branch in Oshodi, one of the major business hubs in Lagos, is in line with the FCMB’s strategic expansion drive and commitment towards bringing its banking services closer to the doorsteps of more people and businesses, while promoting financial inclusion in Nigeria.

“The FCMB’s new outlet in Oshodi has been equipped with unique physical and technological infrastructure that will ensure convenient transactions and sundry financial service delivery to existing and potential customers in a relaxed and tranquil environment.”

Speaking at the inauguration, the Executive Director, Business Development of FCMB, Mrs Bukola Smith, reiterated the bank’s commitment to strategically grow its network and retail business to positively impact the individual and business aspirations of its increasing customer base and the general public.
