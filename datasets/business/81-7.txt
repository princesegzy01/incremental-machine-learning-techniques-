‘FG committed to addressing poverty’
Ifeanyi Onuba, Abuja

The Federal Government is committed to addressing the level of poverty in the country through its social investment schemes.

The Special Adviser to the President on Social Investments, Maryam Uwais said this during an award from Schwab Foundation, a sister organisation of the World Economic Forum WEF.

A statement from the National Safety Investment Office said Uwais joined 40 other individuals selected from different countries across the world to receive the award in recognition of their innovative approach and potential for global impact.

The 2019 awardees were formally inaugurated during the World Economic Forum’s Sustainable Development impact summit held on Monday, in New York, United States.
