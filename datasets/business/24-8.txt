Minister, LCCI boss, others for Lagos trade fair
Samuel Awoyinfa

The Minister of Industry, Trade and Investment, Otunba Niyi Adebayo, the President of the Lagos Chamber of Commerce and Industry, Mr Babatunde Ruwase, and other stakeholders will be playing major roles during the forthcoming Lagos International Trade Fair scheduled to start on Friday.

Giving insights into what to expect at the fair, the Managing Director, MD Perspective Nigeria Limited, a firm involved in events packaging as well business support services, Mrs Morenike Dele-Alimi, in a press release made available to our correspondent, said Nigerian businesses should look forward to a very rewarding experience.

Her company, in partnership with the United Asia International Exhibition Group Company and others will play host to over 1,000 exhibitors drawn from local and international participants, according to the statement.

Local businesses, she stressed, would benefit from the special business-to-business fair.
