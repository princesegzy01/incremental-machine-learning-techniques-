Stanbic IBTC promotes investment
Nike Popoola

The Chief Executive, Stanbic IBTC Bank Plc, Dr Demola Sogunle, says it is committed to showcasing investment potential in Nigeria.

In a statement, he spoke during the bank’s 10th edition of Standard Bank West Africa Investors’ conference with the theme, “Prism of possibilities.”

The conference brought together foreign and local institutional investors, fund managers, regulators and policy makers, bankers, businessmen and captains of industry among others, to engage and explore growth potentials and opportunities in Nigeria.

Sogunle said, “The West Africa Investors’ conference serves as a veritable platform to match investors to investment opportunities. The idea is to be able to showcase the potential investments inherent in Nigeria to individual and institutional investors, who are looking to invest in the different sectors in the Nigerian economy.”

He added that the conference dedicated its panel session to FinTechs due to their significant role in Nigerian economy.

The chief executive stated, “The Nigerian financial system as we know it is being disrupted with the introduction of innovative products and services. There is also the need for fintechs to see potential areas of alignment with financial institutions.”
