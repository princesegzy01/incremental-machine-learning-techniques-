FBNQuest offers tips on wealth management
Nike Popoola

FBNQuest Merchant Bank, the investment banking and asset management business of FBN Holdings Plc, says it is important to secure wealth through the services of professional financial institutions to guarantee successive generational wealth transfer and stable investment growth.

The bank stated this at its Wealth Management Customer Forum, where it shared an in-depth analysis of the impact of long-term wealth preservation and inter-generational wealth transfer.

The Managing Director/Chief Executive Officer, FBNQuest Merchant Bank, Kayode Akinkugbe, reaffirmed the bank’s commitment to helping customers build more long-term and strong investment portfolios through structured wealth management services.

He said, “We work with our clients to provide tailored investments solutions to help build, sustain and transfer wealth across generations.

“Over the years, we have actively partnered with both individual and institutional clients to grow financial assets and investment portfolios in line with varying wealth management objectives. We ensure that we seek investments that are safe, liquid and profitable for long-term sustainability.”
