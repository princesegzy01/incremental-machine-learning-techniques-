Treasury bills oversubscribed by N512.22bn in October
Nike Popoola

The Nigerian treasury bills auctioned in October was oversubscribed by N512.22bn.

The Debt Management Office disclosed that N121.88bn treasury bills of N5.84bn 91-day, N3.5bn 182-day and N112.53bn 374-day tenors were auctioned on October 16.

The auction results showed a total subscription level of N634.11bn which comprised N9.38bn for the 91-day tenor, N38.08bn for the 182-day tenor and N586.63bn for the 364-day tenor.

A total of N121.88bn was allotted which comprised N5.84bn for the 91-day tenor, N3.5bn for the 182-day tenor and N112.53bn for the 364-day tenor.
