Air Liquide seeks N2.07bn capital raise
Feyisayo Popoola

Air Liquide Plc has revealed its intentions to raise N2.07bn capital through a rights issue on the NASD OTC Securities Exchange.

The NASD said in a notice on Thursday that Air Liquide, had through its stockbroker, Stanbic IBTC Stockbrokers Limited, submitted an application for the approval and listing of a rights issue of 414 million units of 50 kobo each at N5 per share.

It said the rights issue would be on the basis of 23 new ordinary shares for every 10 ordinary shares held.

According to the notice, the qualification date for the rights issue was October 15.
