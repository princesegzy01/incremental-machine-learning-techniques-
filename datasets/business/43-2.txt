Pensions Alliance gets acting managing director
Pension Fund Administrator, one of the Pensions Alliance Limited, says it has appointed the Executive Director of Finance and Operations, Mr Godwin Onoro, as its acting managing director.

A statement signed by the Company Secretary of the PFA, Folasade Modebelu, said he would lead the company, until a new substantive managing director would be announced.

The statement said the erstwhile Managing Director, Mrs Morohunke Bammeke, concluded her term with effect from October 3, 2019.
