FRSC, others to discuss truck drivers’ conduct
The Corps Marshal, Federal Road Safety Corps, Dr Boboye Oyeyemi, will on Wednesday October 9 lead other major stakeholders to a discussion forum in Lagos where the issue of truck drivers’ conduct will be the focus.

The event is the 2019 edition of the haulage and logistics magazine annual conference and exhibition.

The theme of this year’s event was given by the organisers in a statement as “Developing a stakeholders’ approach to driver management and professionalism for safety in cargo transportation in Nigeria”.

It said that HAULMACE 2019 was aimed at developing an approach to truck drivers’ management in order to achieve maximum safety and professionalism in cargo transportation.

It stated, “The conduct of truck drivers seen as unruly and unprofessional in Nigeria has been of serious concern to all stakeholders in cargo transportation.

“The aim of the annual event is to provide a platform for advocacy and resolution of pressing issues affecting the growth and development of the Nigerian haulage and logistics industry.”

It added that the forum would try to inculcate the culture of safety among stakeholders in the product distribution value chain and create a professional career path for Nigerian truck drivers and their assistants.

The statement added that the stakeholders in the haulage and logistics sector would be expected to develop a practicable approach to drivers’ management and promote professionalism in truck driving.
