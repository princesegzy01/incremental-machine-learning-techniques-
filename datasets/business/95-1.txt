NPF MfB to raise N3.4bn in rights issue
Feyisayo Popoola

NPF Microfinance Bank Plc is seeking to raise N3.4bn in a proposed rights issue.

The Nigerian Stock Exchange said in a notice to dealing members that the bank had through its stockbroker, Regency Asset Management Limited, submitted an application for the approval and listing of a rights issue of 2,286,657,766 ordinary shares of 50 kobo each.

The notice said the new shares would be issued at N1.50 per share on the basis of one new ordinary share for every one ordinary share held.

It added that the qualification date for the rights issue was Thursday, September 12, 2019.
