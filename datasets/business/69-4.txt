Cars45 unveils emergency service for road users
Maureen Ihua-Maduenyi

Automotive trading platform, Car45, has unveiled an emergency service to provide quick response to road users.

The Vice President, Customer and Dealer Experience, Cars45, Oluseyi Folaranmi, said the service was as a result of the firm’s commitment to provide customers with essential automotive services that would make their lives easier and more productive.

He explained that the ‘Cars45 Emergency Service’ would deliver professional, quick-response services for stranded consumers at prompt notice.

Folaranmi said the service was aimed at bringing relief and comfort to road users on every journey, providing roadside assistance coverage across issues that ranged from jump-starting a vehicle should the battery lose charge.

He said it would also provide services such as replacing a flat tyre with a spare, delivering fuel to stranded vehicles, towing off disabled vehicles and lockout services for keys that had been locked inside the vehicle.

“The emergency service is testament to Cars45’s efforts at improving customer experience and bringing relief to road users on their journey,” he added.

According to him, the firm’s commitment is to consistently improve the nation’s automotive industry with extensive investment in developing solutions that make life better and deliver convenience to users on every trip.

He said the service was being piloted in the Ikoyi, Victoria Island and Lekki Phase 1 areas of Lagos, adding that the firm had provided telephone numbers that consumers could dial for roadside assistance.
