May & Baker appoints Daisy Danjuma as chairman
May & Baker Nigeria Plc has announced the appointment of Senator Daisy Danjuma as its new chairman.

The company said in a notice to the Nigerian Stock Exchange on Wednesday that the appointment came on the heels of the retirement of Lt. Gen. Theophilus Danjuma (retd) as the director and the chairman of the board of directors.

It said Danjuma’s appointment took effect on September 27, 2019.

May & Baker said Danjuma was first appointed to the board of directors on July 30, 1999, and reappointed on May 30, 2019.

It stated that Danjuma was a law graduate from the Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria and had over four decades post-call experience, having been called to the Nigerian bar in 1977.

The statement read in part, “Danjuma has worked as a State Counsel in Lagos State Ministry of Justice and was the pioneer legal counsel to the Legal Aid Council

“She was company secretary/legal adviser to the Nigerian Television Authority from l982 to 1992 and was elected Senator of the Federal Republic of Nigeria in 2003.”

The statement said Daisy currently held the positions of chairman, board of trustees, Lagos Public Interest Law Partnership and executive vice chairman, South Atlantic Petroleum Limited.
