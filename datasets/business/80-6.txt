Adebayo, Dangote top list of IoD conference dignitaries
Anna Okon

The Minister of Industry, Trade and Investment, Otunba Adeniyi Adebayo and the Chairman of Dangote Group, Alhaji Aliko Dangote are among the dignitaries that are billed to discuss the African Continental free Trade Agreement during the Institute of Directors Nigeria’s annual Directors’ conference in Abuja.

According to a statement from the institute, Adebayo has been chosen as the keynote speaker while Dangote will be the chairman at the conference which will be declare open by the Vice- President, Prof Yemi Osinbajo.

The statement signed by the Director- General and Chief Executive Officer, IoD, Bamidele Alimi, added that the conference with the theme, ‘African Continental Free Trade Agreement,’ would also feature the presence of other top participants including the Chairman, Economic Advisory Committee of the Federal Government, Dr Doyin Salami, the Special Adviser to the President on Ease of Doing Business, Dr Jumoke Oduwole, the President, Manufacturers Association of Nigeria, Ahmed Mansur, Partner, The Law Union; Mr Desmond Guobadia, the Director- General, Infrastructure Concession Regulatory Commission, Chidi lzuwah and the Acting Managing Director of Nigeria Export Processing Zones Authority, Terhemba Nongo.
