Buhari’s aide to headline Autoprenuer launch today
Samuel Awoyinfa

Nigeria’s leading automotive trading platform, Cars45, will on Thursday (today) inaugurate an innovative empowerment initiative, Cars45 Autopreneur Programme.

According to a statement issued by the company on Wednesday, the platform is designed as a framework for poverty alleviation scheme that will empower business growth and profitability.

The Vice President, Retail Services, Cars45, John Egwu, said the event would be headlined by a keynote presentation by the Special Adviser to President Muhammadu Buhari on Ease of Doing Business, Dr Jumoke Oduwole.

Oduwole also doubles as the Secretary, Presidential Enabling Business Environment Council.
