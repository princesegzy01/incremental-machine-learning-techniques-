NASD holds stakeholders forum
Feyisayo Popoola

NASD Plc hosted its stakeholders to a session on blockchain and cryptocurrency assets primer.

The exchange said in a statement that the forum was facilitated by Pind Foundation’s Enitan Odogun.

Odogun described technology as an important tool to humanity and a key tool for excellent service delivery and security for the capital market.

He said as technology evolved, the capital market community must also move along with the times.

The statement read in part, “As a forward thinking and responsible trading platform, NASD is committed to delivering a transparent, secure and convenient service.

“Since it commenced operations in 2013, NASD has continued to develop new opportunities in the Nigerian Capital Market. It is also committed to its promise of creating new investment opportunities for investors using technology and the most problem solving innovative ideas.”

It added that the forum provided an opportunity for stockbrokers, members of the Nigerian capital market community and the media to ask questions and gain insights on the activities of the Exchange while sharing their thoughts on key issues affecting the market.
