Chemstar Group appoints chairman, others
Chemstar Group has named Mr Aderemi Awode as the Chairman/Chief Executive Officer of the group.

Until his appointment, Awode was the Group Managing Director and Chief Executive Officer of the company’s arm, Chemstar Paints Industry (Nigeria) Ltd.

Awode, a graduate of Chemistry from The Polytechnic, Ibadan, is a paint chemist with passion for paints technology in the areas of building paints, industrial coatings, marine coatings, automotive paints and wood finishes.

Also, the former Deputy Managing Director, Mr Adedayo Paseda, has been appointed as the Group Managing Director of Chemstar Paints and its subsidiaries.

Paseda, an alumnus of Obafemi Awolowo University, holds a Bachelor of Science in Chemical Engineering and a master’s degree in Business Administration from the University of Lagos.

According to the statement, other changes announced by the board show that Mr Fola Laguda, a former General Manager Sales and Marketing, who now becomes the Executive Director, Sales and Marketing.

Also, a former General Manager, Strategy & Business Development, Dr Tunde Olaniya, has been appointed the Executive Director, C-PIN Industries Limited.

The board, which said that the new appointments had since taken immediate effect, expressed optimism in the new appointees that they would deploy their wealth of experience and expertise in the development of the group and the nation’s economy at large.
