Nigeria hosts AFCAC, Singapore CAA safety training
Maureen Ihua-Maduenyi

The Federal Government through the Nigerian Civil Aviation Authority has hosted the 18th African Civil Aviation Commission and Civil Aviation Authority of Singaporeâ€™s on-site cabin safety oversight course.

The NCAA said Nigeria was selected to host participants from different African countries after due consideration which included its safety record of airline operations.

This course was conducted under the framework of the memorandum of understanding between the AFCAC and the CAA Singapore.

The General Manager, Public Relations, NCAA, Mr Sam Adurogboye, said in a statement on Tuesday that the course commenced from September 9 and ended September 13, 2019 at the training centre of the NCAA headquarters Annex.

He said the objective was to provide participants with a good understanding of critical areas of cabin safety oversight.
