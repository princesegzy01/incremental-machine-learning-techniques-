Automobile firms set for Abuja motor fair
Samuel Awoyinfa

Giants in the automobile industry in Nigeria are set to showcase their latest wonders-on-wheel at the International Conference Centre Annex, Abuja, venue of the 20th edition of the Abuja International Motor Fair.

According to the Chairman of the event’s organising committee, Mr Ifeanyichukwu Agwu, the fair will be a landmark in auto show business in the entire West African sub-region. The event holds from October 28 to November 2, 2019.

He said participants and visitors would have a rewarding experience, adding that major vehicle manufacturers and dealers in Nigeria would be participating in the fair.

“Notable banks and insurance companies, major oil companies, manufacturers and dealers in auto accessories have indicated interest and are seriously preparing to participate in the event,” he said.

He commended the interest shown by these reputable companies, noting that providing a platform for all segments of the automobile sector to showcase their best and latest and thereby fast-tracking the development of the sector in Nigeria.
