Buhari reappoints Adeyemi, Adeleye as SAs
Anna Okon

President Muhammadu Buhari has approved the re-appointment of Mr Segun Adeyemi and Mr Williams Adeleye as his Special Assistants (Media) attached to the Office of the Minister of Information and Culture.

A statement by the Chief Press Secretary to the Minister of Information and Culture, Joe Mutah, on Tuesday, their letters which were signed by the Secretary to the Government of the Federation, Mr Boss Mustapha, indicated that the appointment took effect from September 6, 2019.

Adeyemi and Adeleye served in the same capacity between November 2015 and May 2019.
