FG to review infrastructure at airports – Minister
Maureen Ihua-Maduenyi

The Minister of Aviation, Hadi Sirika, has said the Federal Government plans to upgrade infrastructure in airports across the country to boost safety.

Sirika said stakeholders had identified constant review of infrastructure and aviation practices as the way forward.

The minister, at a summit and awards ceremony organised by the Association of Foreign Airlines and Representatives in Nigeria in Lagos on Wednesday, said the government was determined to create the right atmosphere for aviation business to thrive.

Sirika, who was represented by his chief of staff, Dr Ibrahim Idris, urged stakeholders in the aviation industry to uphold safety as indicated in the theme of the summit, ‘unlocking potential in the aviation industry through safety’.
