Lalong restates commitment to economic growth
James Abraham, Jos

Plateau State Governor, Simon Lalong, on Monday reiterated his administration’s commitment to lead the state to ecoomic growth and prosperity.

He said this while declaring open the Jos 2019 trade fair holding at the PoloField in the state capital .

Our correspondent gathered that the trade fair with the theme ‘Trade fair, a platform for promoting tourism potential on the Plateau’ was the 8th edition organised in the state.

The governor said that it was the desire of his administration to “ not only restore the glory of our beloved state, but also build an economy that will provide opportunities and support the entrepreneurial spirit of the Plateau man, woman and the youth.”
