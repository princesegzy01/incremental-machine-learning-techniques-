Shareholders approve International Breweries rights issue
Feyisayo Popoola

Shareholders of International Breweries Plc have approved the company’s rights issue.

The firm said in a notice to the Nigerian Stock Exchange on Thursday that the approval was given at the Annual General Meeting on Tuesday.

It said shareholders approved the rights issue as proposed by the board of directors and authorised the board to take all steps required to give effect to the resolutions as approved.

The notice read in part, “Following this approval, the board of directors discussed the forthcoming rights issue and the proposed terms thereof.

“A rights issue price of N9 per share was approved.”

It said further details on the rights issue would be provided in due course.
