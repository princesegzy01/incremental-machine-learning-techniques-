SO&U appoints Adefila as Chief Operating Officer
Anna Okon

Nigeria’s marketing communications company, SO&U has announced the appointment of Mrs Biodun Adefila as its Chief Operating Officer.

The company said until her recent elevation, Adefila was the company’s Executive Director, Brand Management, responsible for formulation and implementation of strategies for execution of its brand management initiatives.

It said “she is experienced and honed her skills at some of the country’s leading advertising companies, adding that she had been an integral part of the SO&U success story for more than two decades.”

Adefila according to SO&U holds an MBA in Marketing from the University of Liverpool and a Certificate in Advanced Management from the Lagos Business School.
